Company Policy - Internal Guidelines

1. Working Hours:
- Employees are expected to work 8 hours a day, Monday through Friday.
- Flexible timings are allowed with prior approval from the team lead.

2. Remote Work:
- Remote work is allowed up to 3 days a week.
- Employees must be available on Slack and attend all scheduled meetings.

3. Leave Policy:
- 24 annual paid leaves per year.
- All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 days in advance.

4. Security Guidelines:
- All company data must be accessed through a VPN.
- Passwords should be changed every 90 days.
- Sharing login credentials is strictly prohibited.

5. Expense Reimbursements:
- Submit valid receipts through the Finance tool.
- Approvals are required from both the team lead and finance department.
